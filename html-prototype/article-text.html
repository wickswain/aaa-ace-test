Getting around

The 20-stop SIA Hop-on bus with recorded narration arrives every 30 minutes from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. A day pass gets you unlimited rides. Singapore Airlines passengers with an air ticket or boarding pass pay duscount prices.

011-6506558-6877; siahopon.com

If you want to commute like a local, the Singapore Tourist Pass offers unlimited rides on the public transport system, Purchase a one-, two- or three-day pass.
